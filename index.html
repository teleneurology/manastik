about us

A platform to connect with neurologists, developers, related healthcare professionals and dementia patients as well as caregivers

Manastik is a developer community wherein developers can join and learn, enjoy coding and watch closely how ideas are turned into real-life projects. At the same time, we at Manastik, engage with neurologists, neuropsychologists, psychiatrists, SLPs and other Neurorehabilitation professionals to undersand the needs and problems within the ecosystem of Neurology. We are committed towards fulfilling those needs for the greater good.

Initial History Taking

This project solves the problem of initial history taking digitally and conversation of the neurologist and neuropsychologist with patients.

Cloud storage of Medical Records

Medical reports pertaining to but not limited to Neurology such as PET Scan, CT Scan, MRI, MRA, EEG, etc. can be stored on cloud, sent to the neurologists as well as healthcare professionals and accessed by the patients anywhere on priority.

about us

A platform to connect neurologists, developers, healthcare professionals and caregivers

Manastik is a developer community aimed at bridging the gap between technology and neurology by connecting developers with neurologists. It is a platform where developers can join and learn, enjoy coding and watch closely how ideas are turned into real-life projects. We at Manastik, engage with Neurologists, Neuropsychologists, Psychiatrists, SLPs and other Neurorehabilitation professionals to undersand the needs and problems within the ecosystem of Neurology. We are committed towards fulfilling those needs for the greater good. We bring in our expertise in business & technology to create innovative solutions and tackle the problems.

Our projects

DADt-Dementia Active Disease Tracking

Dementia Active Diagnosis and Tracking (DADt) is an online app that helps with neurological evaluations and cognitive testing for people suffering from dementia. It helps doctors diagnose dementia better and helps caregivers track progression of dementia.

Initial History Taking

This project solves the problem of initial history taking digitally and conversation of the neurologist and neuropsychologist with patients.

Cloud storage of Medical Records

Medical reports pertaining to but not limited to Neurology such as PET Scan, CT Scan, MRI, MRA, EEG, etc. can be stored on cloud, sent to the neurologists as well as healthcare professionals and accessed by the patients anywhere on priority.
